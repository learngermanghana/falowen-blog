## Where can you learn German in Ghana or Nigeria?
If you search online, you will see many options. The challenge is choosing one that actually helps you progress.

A good German learning platform should give you:
- a clear study plan
- regular speaking and writing practice
- corrections and tutor feedback
- exam preparation support (Goethe pathway)

---

## What African learners should look for before joining
Before you choose any course, check these points:

### 1) Structured roadmap
Can you see what to study each day and each week?

### 2) Real communication practice
Does the platform train you to speak and write, not only read grammar notes?

### 3) Feedback system
Who corrects your mistakes and how quickly?

### 4) Exam relevance
If your goal is a certificate, does the learning path align with Goethe-style expectations?
