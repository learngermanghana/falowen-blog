## How to Talk About Your Hobbies in German

Talking about your hobbies is a great way to practice German and get to know others. In this guide, you'll learn useful vocabulary and simple sentences to describe what you like to do in your free time.

---

## Hobby Vocabulary in German

Here are some common hobbies with their German translations:

- **lesen** – to read  
- **reisen** – to travel  
- **kochen** – to cook  
- **tanzen** – to dance  
- **schwimmen** – to swim  
- **Musik hören** – to listen to music  

---

## Simple Sentences to Describe Your Hobbies

Use these sentence structures to talk about your hobbies:

### 1. Ich + verb + gern  
This means "I like to..."  
- Ich lese gern. (I like to read.)  
- Ich tanze gern. (I like to dance.)  

### 2. Mein Hobby ist + noun  
This means "My hobby is..."  
- Mein Hobby ist Kochen. (My hobby is cooking.)  
- Mein Hobby ist Schwimmen. (My hobby is swimming.)  

### 3. In meiner Freizeit + verb ich + gern  
This means "In my free time, I like to..."  
- In meiner Freizeit reise ich gern. (In my free time, I like to travel.)  
- In meiner Freizeit höre ich gern Musik. (In my free time, I like to listen to music.)  

---

## Tips for Practicing

- Start by memorizing the hobby words above.  
- Practice saying simple sentences about your hobbies out loud.  
- Try to ask others about their hobbies using:  
  *Was machst du gern in deiner Freizeit?* (What do you like to do in your free time?)
