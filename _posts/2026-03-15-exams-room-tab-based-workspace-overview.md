## A simple exam space that keeps everything together

Exam preparation can feel stressful when learners jump between many tools.

The **Exams Room** solves this by putting everything in one place. Learners can open the section they need, practice, and move to the next task without getting lost.

---

## What learners see inside the Exams Room

At the top, there is a clear row of tabs:

- tutor
- lesen
- speaking
- writing
- vocab
- hören
- resources
- study
- file

Each tab has one clear purpose, so learners always know where to go.

---

## What each tab is for

- **Tutor**: guided support for exam writing
- **Speaking**: speaking practice tasks
- **Writing**: writing exam tasks
- **Vocab**: vocabulary practice for exams
- **Hören**: listening practice
- **Lesen**: reading practice
- **Resources**: exam materials and support content
- **Study**: study planning and calendar
- **File**: personal exam records

---

## Better level support

Learners can work from **A1 to C1**.

If a learner already has a saved level in their profile, that level is selected automatically. This helps them train at the right difficulty without extra setup.

---

## A clear start page that saves time

The Exams Room overview helps learners start quickly:

- shows current level
- lets them **resume last session**
- gives a **start speaking warm-up** shortcut
- remembers their last section so they can continue later
- shows simple cards that explain each tab

This makes it easy to start learning immediately.

---

## Why this matters

The goal is simple: help learners stay consistent.

When the learning space is clear and easy to use, learners can focus on practice, not navigation. Over time, that leads to better exam confidence and better results.
