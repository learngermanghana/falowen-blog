## 20 Basic German Words Every Student Must Know

Learning German starts with building a strong foundation of essential vocabulary. These **basic German words** are a must for every beginner and will help you navigate daily conversations, understand simple texts, and feel more confident speaking.

### Why Start with Basic German Words?

- They form the building blocks of sentences.
- You’ll recognize them often in conversations and reading.
- Knowing them boosts your confidence and motivation.

---

## 20 Essential Basic German Words

Here are 20 **important beginner vocabulary** words to get you started:

### Common Nouns
- **das Haus** – the house  
- **die Schule** – the school  
- **der Freund / die Freundin** – the friend (male/female)  

### Useful Verbs
- **sein** – to be  
- **haben** – to have  
- **gehen** – to go  

### Everyday Adjectives
- **gut** – good  
- **klein** – small  
- **neu** – new  

### Key Question Words
- **wer?** – who?  
- **was?** – what?  
- **wo?** – where?  

### Basic Prepositions
- **in** – in  
- **auf** – on  
- **mit** – with  

### Simple Connectors
- **und** – and  
- **aber** – but  
- **oder** – or  

---

## Practical Tips for Learning Basic German Words

- **Use flashcards**: Write the German word on one side and the meaning on the other.
- **Practice in context**: Try making simple sentences with each word.
- **Speak aloud**: Pronunciation helps you remember better.
- **Review regularly**: Repetition is key to long-term memory.
