## How to Introduce Yourself in German

Introducing yourself is one of the first and most important skills when learning German. Knowing simple self-introduction sentences helps you start conversations, make friends, and feel confident in new situations.

### Basic Phrases to Introduce Yourself in German

Here are some easy sentences you can use right away:

- **Hallo, ich heiße [Name].**  
  (Hello, my name is [Name].)

- **Ich bin [Name].**  
  (I am [Name].)

- **Mein Name ist [Name].**  
  (My name is [Name].)

- **Ich komme aus [Land/Stadt].**  
  (I come from [country/city].)

- **Ich wohne in [Stadt].**  
  (I live in [city].)

- **Ich bin [Alter] Jahre alt.**  
  (I am [age] years old.)

### Putting It Together: Simple Self-Introductions

Here are some example introductions using the phrases above:

1. Hallo, ich heiße Anna. Ich komme aus Deutschland.  
2. Ich bin Max. Ich wohne in Berlin.  
3. Mein Name ist Julia. Ich bin 25 Jahre alt.  
4. Hallo, ich heiße Tom und ich komme aus Österreich.  
5. Ich bin Lisa. Ich wohne in München und bin 30 Jahre alt.  
6. Mein Name ist Peter. Ich komme aus der Schweiz.

### Tips for Speaking Practice

- Practice saying your name and where you’re from out loud every day.
- Try to memorize one or two sentences so you can introduce yourself naturally.
- Record yourself and listen to improve your pronunciation.
- Use these phrases when meeting new people or in language classes.
