## How to Introduce Yourself in German: Simple Self-Introduction Sentences

Introducing yourself is one of the first steps to start speaking German confidently. In this guide, you'll learn clear and easy sentences to introduce yourself in everyday situations.

### Basic Phrases to Introduce Yourself in German

Here are some simple sentences you can use right away:

- **Ich heiße [Name].**  
  *My name is [Name].*

- **Mein Name ist [Name].**  
  *My name is [Name].*

- **Ich bin [Name].**  
  *I am [Name].*

- **Ich komme aus [Land/Stadt].**  
  *I come from [country/city].*

- **Ich wohne in [Stadt].**  
  *I live in [city].*

- **Ich lerne Deutsch.**  
  *I am learning German.*

### Adding More Details About Yourself

After the basics, you can add simple information about your profession, hobbies, or age:

- **Ich bin Student/Studentin.**  
  *I am a student.*

- **Ich arbeite als [Beruf].**  
  *I work as a [profession].*

- **Ich bin [Alter] Jahre alt.**  
  *I am [age] years old.*

- **In meiner Freizeit spiele ich Fußball.**  
  *In my free time, I play soccer.*

### Tips for Practicing Your Self-Introduction

- Practice saying your sentences out loud slowly at first.  
- Use your own details to make it personal and easier to remember.  
- Record yourself and listen to improve pronunciation.  
- Try introducing yourself to a friend or language partner.
