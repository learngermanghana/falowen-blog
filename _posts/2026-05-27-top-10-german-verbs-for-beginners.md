## Top 10 German Verbs for Beginners

Learning German verbs is essential for building sentences and expressing yourself clearly. Today, we'll focus on the **top 10 German verbs for beginners** that you will use daily. Mastering these will give you a strong foundation for speaking and understanding German.

---

## Why Start with These Verbs?

These verbs are common, versatile, and appear in many everyday situations. Knowing them helps you describe actions, states, and feelings effectively.

---

## The Top 10 German Verbs for Beginners

### 1. Sein (to be)
- Ich **bin** müde. (I am tired.)
- Du **bist** freundlich. (You are friendly.)

### 2. Haben (to have)
- Wir **haben** ein Haus. (We have a house.)
- Sie **hat** zwei Katzen. (She has two cats.)

### 3. Gehen (to go)
- Ich **gehe** zur Schule. (I go to school.)
- Er **geht** nach Hause. (He goes home.)

### 4. Machen (to do/make)
- Was **machst** du? (What are you doing?)
- Ich **mache** meine Hausaufgaben. (I am doing my homework.)

### 5. Sagen (to say)
- Er **sagt** die Wahrheit. (He tells the truth.)
- Was hast du **gesagt**? (What did you say?)

### 6. Kommen (to come)
- Wir **kommen** aus Deutschland. (We come from Germany.)
- Kommst du heute? (Are you coming today?)

### 7. Sehen (to see)
- Ich **sehe** den Hund. (I see the dog.)
- Sie **sieht** gut aus. (She looks good.)

### 8. Essen (to eat)
- Ich **esse** gern Obst. (I like to eat fruit.)
- Wir **essen** um 18 Uhr. (We eat at 6 p.m.)

### 9. Trinken (to drink)
- Trinkst du Wasser? (Do you drink water?)
- Er **trinkt** Kaffee. (He drinks coffee.)

### 10. Lernen (to learn)
- Ich **lerne** Deutsch. (I am learning German.)
- Sie **lernen** jeden Tag. (They learn every day.)

---

## Tips for Learning German Verbs

- Practice conjugating these verbs in different persons (ich, du, er/sie/es, wir, ihr, sie).
- Use flashcards with the infinitive on one side and example sentences on the other.
- Try to make simple sentences with each verb daily.
- Listen for these verbs in German conversations, songs, or videos.
