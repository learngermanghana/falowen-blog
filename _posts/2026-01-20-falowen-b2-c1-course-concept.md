## What changes from B2 to C1 (same structure, higher expectations)
### B2 (advanced level target)
B2 is aligned with **Goethe-Zertifikat B2** expectations:
- clear structure and argumentation
- confident everyday + semi-formal language
- solid writing in B2-appropriate formats
- speaking that is organized and easy to follow

### C1 (proficient level target)
C1 is aligned with **Goethe-Zertifikat C1** expectations:
- more nuanced, precise vocabulary
- stronger cohesion and advanced connectors
- higher writing standards (tone, register, style)
- speaking that sounds flexible, natural, and well-reasoned
