## How to Ask Simple Questions in German

Asking questions is one of the most important skills when learning a new language. In German, question words help you gather information and start conversations. Today, we'll focus on some common **German question words** and how to use them correctly.

### Key German Question Words

Here are four basic question words you should know:

- **Wo?** — Where?  
- **Was?** — What?  
- **Wer?** — Who?  
- **Wann?** — When?  

These words often come at the beginning of a question, followed by the verb and then the subject.

### Examples with Question Words

Let's look at how to use each question word in a simple sentence:

- **Wo bist du?**  
  *Where are you?*

- **Was machst du?**  
  *What are you doing?*

- **Wer ist das?**  
  *Who is that?*

- **Wann kommst du?**  
  *When are you coming?*

- **Woher kommst du?**  
  *Where do you come from?*  
  *(Note: "Woher" means "from where.")*

- **Was möchtest du essen?**  
  *What would you like to eat?*

### Tips for Practicing Question Words

- Start by memorizing the question words and their meanings.
- Practice forming simple questions aloud, using the examples above as a guide.
- Try to answer your own questions in German to build conversation skills.
- Remember that the verb usually comes right after the question word in German questions.
